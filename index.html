MakeX Robotics Competitions

During the Maker Faire 2019 in Rome I took part in the organization of the first MakeX Robotics Competitions in Italy, in which numerous national and international teams took part.

I was also a competition judge and I provided the RobocupNetwork software and referee training to all the referees.

I also communicated with the Chinese Committee for the purpose of making a correct organization and management of the competitions.
